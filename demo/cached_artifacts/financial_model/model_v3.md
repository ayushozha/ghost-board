## Key Assumptions
- Average subscription price of $1000 per month per customer
- Customer growth rate of 10% per month
- Gross margin improvement as operations scale
- Achieving product-market fit within the first year

## Risks
- Regulatory changes affecting business model
- Increased competition in the fintech space
- Longer-than-expected sales cycles
- Customer churn due to evolving industry needs
