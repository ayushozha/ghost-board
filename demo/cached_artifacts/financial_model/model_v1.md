# Financial Model v1

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Revenue Y1 | $250,000 |
| Revenue Y3 | $2,000,000 |
| Burn Rate | $50,000/mo |
| Runway | 12 months |
| Funding Req | $1,500,000 |
| CAC | $500 |
| LTV | $4,000 |
| Gross Margin | 75% |

## Key Assumptions
- Average revenue per customer per month is $200
- Initial customer base is 20 companies in the first month, growing by 10% each month
- Operational expenses grow by 5% quarterly
- Free tier users convert to paid users at a rate of 15% within 6 months
- Funding will be secured within the first 6 months to maintain runway

## Risks
- Regulatory changes that increase compliance costs
- Higher than expected churn rate
- Increased competition driving down prices
- Delay in securing additional funding
