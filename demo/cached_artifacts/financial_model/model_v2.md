# Financial Model v2

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Revenue Y1 | $500,000 |
| Revenue Y3 | $3,000,000 |
| Burn Rate | $80,000/mo |
| Runway | 12 months |
| Funding Req | $1,500,000 |
| CAC | $5,000 |
| LTV | $25,000 |
| Gross Margin | 70% |

## Key Assumptions
- Average subscription price is $1000 per month per customer.
- Customer churn rate is 5% annually.
- Annual growth rate in customer base is 100%.
- Initial customer acquisition starts at 5 customers in month 1 with consistent growth.
- Operating expenses grow at 10% per annum.

## Risks
- High customer acquisition costs might impact profitability.
- Compliance changes may necessitate frequent updates to the platform.
- Competition from established players in the data privacy sector.
- Potential data breaches affecting reputation and customer trust.
