# Compliance Report v1

**Risk Level:** HIGH

## Regulations Checked
- Electronic Fund Transfer Act (EFTA) and Regulation E
- Bank Secrecy Act (BSA) and FinCEN regulations
- Securities Exchange Act and SEC regulations
- Federal Trade Commission (FTC) regulations
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- State-specific financial and data protection laws

## Blockers

### [CRITICAL] Consumer Protection
Failure to comply with EFTA and Regulation E requirements, such as providing necessary disclosures and error resolution procedures, can result in significant penalties.
**Citations:**
- https://www.consumerfinance.gov/compliance/compliance-resources/deposit-accounts-resources/electronic-fund-transfers/

**Action:** Ensure that all services align with EFTA and Regulation E by implementing comprehensive consumer protection measures, including clear disclosures and robust error resolution processes.

### [HIGH] Anti-Money Laundering (AML) Compliance
Non-compliance with BSA and FinCEN regulations, including failure to implement effective AML programs and report suspicious activities, can lead to severe legal consequences.
**Citations:**
- https://www.fincen.gov/news/news-releases/fincen-issues-alert-fraud-schemes-involving-deepfake-media-targeting-financial

**Action:** Develop and maintain a robust AML program that includes customer due diligence, transaction monitoring, and timely reporting of suspicious activities.

### [HIGH] Data Privacy
Non-compliance with GDPR requirements, such as failing to protect personal data and not obtaining proper consent, can result in substantial fines and reputational damage.
**Citations:**
- https://gdpr-info.eu/

**Action:** Implement data protection measures in line with GDPR, including data minimization, secure processing, and obtaining explicit consent from users.

### [MEDIUM] State Regulations
Failure to adhere to state-specific financial and data protection laws can lead to legal challenges and fines.
**Citations:**
- https://www.nascus.org/cfpb-summaries/bulletin-2022-02-compliance-bulletin-on-the-efta-compulsory-use-prohibition-and-government-benefit-accounts/

**Action:** Conduct a thorough review of relevant state laws and ensure compliance with all applicable regulations.
