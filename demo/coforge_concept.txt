CoForge: AI cofounder matching platform using behavioral analysis and startup compatibility scoring. Analyzes founder personality traits, working styles, skill gaps, and domain expertise to suggest optimal cofounder pairings. Uses structured interviews, reference checks, and work-style simulations to generate compatibility scores. Target market: solo technical founders at pre-seed stage looking for business cofounders, and vice versa. Business model: freemium with premium matching ($99/month for priority matching and verified profiles). Key differentiators: behavioral compatibility scoring (not just skill matching), simulated collaboration exercises, reference-backed trust scores.
