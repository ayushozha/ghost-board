Anchrix: AI-powered identity verification and regulatory compliance platform for fintech startups. Combines biometric verification (face + liveness detection), document authentication (passport, driver's license, utility bills), and real-time regulatory monitoring across CFPB, FinCEN, SEC, and state-level regulations. The platform provides a single API for KYC/AML compliance with automatic updates when regulations change, reducing compliance costs by 80% and onboarding time from days to minutes. Target market: seed-to-Series-B fintech companies that can't afford a full compliance team. Business model: usage-based SaaS pricing with a free tier for startups under 100 verifications/month.
