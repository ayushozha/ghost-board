DevPulse: AI-powered developer productivity analytics platform for engineering managers. Integrates with GitHub, GitLab, Jira, and Slack to provide real-time insights into team velocity, code review bottlenecks, deployment frequency, and developer satisfaction. Uses ML to identify burnout risks and suggest workflow optimizations. Privacy-first design with no individual developer tracking - only team-level aggregates. Target market: VP Engineering and Engineering Managers at Series A-C startups (50-500 engineers). Business model: per-seat SaaS ($15/developer/month) with enterprise tier for custom integrations. Key differentiator: actionable recommendations not just dashboards, with an AI coach that suggests specific process changes based on DORA metrics and team patterns.
