LearnLoop: Adaptive AI tutoring engine for K-12 math education. Generates personalized problem sets that adapt in real-time to student performance, identifies specific misconception patterns (not just right/wrong), and provides Socratic-style hints that guide discovery rather than giving answers. Features: common core standards alignment, teacher dashboard with class-level analytics, parent progress reports, gamification with mastery-based progression. Target market: school districts (500+ students) and supplemental education companies. Business model: per-student SaaS ($5/student/month for districts, $15/month for individual families). Key differentiator: misconception-aware tutoring that identifies WHY a student is struggling, not just THAT they are.
